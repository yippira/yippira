What Drives Me

There must be a reason to want to wake up everyday

As humans, we reap the ability to use technology to better our lives, to chase after more. However, in chasing more, we place immense stress on ourselves, increase our workload faster than what technology can replace. However, the process of automation continues and more of our manual processes are replaced at an incredible rate. As a developer, I want to be part of the builders that made life easier for the rest of us.
